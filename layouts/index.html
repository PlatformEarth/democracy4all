Jean Pierre Bekolo

#imagine

Throughout human history, stories have been central to our existence, the signal within the noise, recognisable patterns in which we find meaning and give sense to our world. The power of stories What make us human If stories themselves are universal, the way we tell them changes with the technology at hand and every new medium has given rise to new forms of narrative. With the arrival of the internet, our stories broke loose from their linear boundaries, becoming hyperlinked storylines, delivered in mutable and complex formats (audio, video and text). An open communication channel where participation is not only possible but expected and collective imagination can reach its full potential.

Henning Dietrich

#futurelaw

What is a smart contract, and how can we democratise the process of creating the laws and code that govern our every day lives in an increasingly technologically driven society? Henning Dietrich, the founder of Lexon introduces a new way to code smart contracts. Using natural language processing, Lexon enables, non-coders, lawyers, philosophers, hackers and students of the future to create decentralised code (smart contracts) on the blockchain.
